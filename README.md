EyeSpy is a luxury travel safety platform designed to help travelers feel more secure in unfamiliar hotel rooms and short-term rentals. The platform uses a guided room scan to identify possible safety concerns, including suspicious devices, broken locks, exposed wiring, missing detectors, and other room risks. EyeSpy turns those findings into an easy-to-understand safety report with voice-guided feedback and shareable documentation. By combining intelligent visual analysis, device detection, and verified reporting, EyeSpy gives travelers a faster and smarter way to inspect a room before settling in.
